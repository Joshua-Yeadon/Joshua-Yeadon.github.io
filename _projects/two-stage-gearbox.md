## Requirement

1.5 kW input, 4300 rpm to 1400 rpm output, overall ratio about 3.07. The important work was resolving the gearbox as a complete system, not only modelling gears.

<figure>
  <img src="/assets/images/project-details/gear-process.jpg" alt="Gearbox process image showing Autodesk Inventor, calculations, standards, SKF, ISO, and SolidWorks logos">
  <figcaption>The process combined CAD, calculation, standards, supplier data, and manufacturing communication.</figcaption>
</figure>

## System

Gears, shafts, bearings, seals, keys, and housing were developed together so the internal load path and assembly made sense.

<figure>
  <img src="/assets/images/project-details/gear-section.jpg" alt="Section view of two-stage gearbox CAD assembly showing gear sets, shafts, and bearing positions">
  <figcaption>Section views make the design reviewable: gear sets, centre distances, shafts, and bearing support are visible.</figcaption>
</figure>

## Output

- Ratio split across two practical stages.
- Gear and shaft checks supported by CAD-led calculations.
- Component selection around bearings, seals, and keys.
- MBD and manufacturing setup included in the final communication.
