This is a reusable format for a short technical reflection.

## Context

One or two sentences explaining the system or situation.

## Decision

What changed, what was selected, or what was rejected.

## Evidence

Point to a test, calculation, image, CAD detail, or prototype observation.

## Takeaway

One practical sentence about what you learned.
